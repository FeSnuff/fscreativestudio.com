Transform your kitchen without the high cost of replacement

Why Choose Cabinet Refinishing?

  • Save 50-75% compared to cabinet replacement
  • Complete transformation in just days, not weeks
  • Eco-friendly alternative to throwing away cabinets
  • Factory-quality finish with durable, long-lasting results
  • Custom color matching to your exact specifications
  • Specialized techniques perfected over 30 years
  • Minimal disruption to your daily routine
  • Breathe new life into outdated or worn cabinets

Our cabinet refinishing process includes thorough cleaning, professional sanding, primer application, multiple coats of premium paint or stain, and a protective topcoat. The result? Cabinets that look brand new and will last for years to come.
